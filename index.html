LCM Barber Co

I opened this barbershop on September 8, 2015 in hopes of bringing much needed barbers to this area. I live in Schertz Texas and thought that opening a shop in New Braunfels was a better fit for me and my family. My wife Debbie works on Saturdays cutting hair and my son Leon Jr. will be cutting here after he finishes barber school in April of 2016. My other son and my daughter may cut hair at the shop in the future making this a family business, hence the name L.C.M. BARBER CO., named after my 3 kids Leon, Christian, and Mary-Elizabeth, but only time will tell. So pleases stop by and enjoy the atmosphere, where everyone is welcome. Thank You. - Leon Hernandez

Services

Family Friendly Atmosphere

We greet all of our customers with a smile. As a customer you can expect a clean, comfortable and family friendly atmosphere. We try to deliver the experience tailored to the needs, requirements and budget of our clients. So, come and try! Make sure you are in the right place. Don't miss your chance to look your best!

Clean Cuts

We take tremendous pride in our work and perpetuating the fine art of classic barbering. Our strength and differentiation is manifest in the details: how intently we listen to what you want, how well the hair is tapered, how straight the neckline is, how even the sideburns are. You can’t find a finer men’s haircut in New Braunfels. We would love the opportunity to be your local barber of choice.

Beard and Stache Grooming

Conquer your whisker war. If you have a beard or goatee, your stylist can trim and shape it for you. You'll wonder why you mess with this at home.
