About Us

I opened this barbershop on September 8, 2015 in hopes of bringing much needed barbers to this area. I live in Schertz Texas and thought that opening a shop in New Braunfels was a better fit for me and my family. My wife Debbie works on Saturdays cutting hair and my son Leon Jr. will be cutting here after he finishes barber school in April of 2016. My other son and my daughter may cut hair at the shop in the future making this a family business, hence the name L.C.M. BARBER CO., named after my 3 kids Leon, Christian, and Mary-Elizabeth, but only time will tell. So pleases stop by and enjoy the atmosphere, where everyone is welcome. Thank You. - Leon Hernandez
